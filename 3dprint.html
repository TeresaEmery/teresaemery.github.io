3D Printed Landscape

3D Model from Photoshop

3D Printed & Hand-painted Model


I started with an idea of creating a 3D model of the San Francisco Bay Area. After failing with other methods, I ended up making this in Photoshop. I started with a satellite image of the Bay Area from NASA and blurred the image slightly and lowered the contrast to soften the tonal values. I also cut away one edge along the water. Photoshop then extruded the photo into a 3D model, lifting lighter areas and depressing dark areas, resulting in a surreal, fantastical hybrid of the actual landscape.
